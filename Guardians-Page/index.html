Sheikah Inventions

The great and fearsome Guardians have ancient origins in Sheikah society. The strong, calculating warriors were developed by Sheikah scientists to defend Hyrule against Calamity Ganon. Little is known about Sheikah technology as the Sheikah people maintain secrecy and allegiance to the rulers of Hyrule. However, Guardians remain as relics of Hyrule's prowess, now afflicted by the calamity that scourges Hyrule.

Author: Caroline Hopkins
